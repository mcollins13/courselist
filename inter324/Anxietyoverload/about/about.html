About

I created Anxiety Overload because anxieties are an everyday problem for me. I find that when I open up about things I’m afraid of, other share their fears or are very understanding of my fears. Sometimes I feel alone in dealing with anxiety issues however I know this isn’t true. Everyone has some kind of crazy irrational fear, like spiders. I want to see who else is willing to share their fears with the world.

Achluophobia (fear of darkness)
I used to not be able to go up on the second floor of my house during the night because it was dark up there. I still don’t like walking home at night.
Acrophobia (fear of heights)
one time, my sister and I took an elevator up to a restaurant at the top of a hotel. When the doors opened all I could see were windows upon windows and the city far below. I couldn’t move no matter how hard I tried. In the end my sister and I went back down in the elevator and we went somewhere else to eat.
Aerophobia (fear of drafts, air swallowing, or airborne noxious substances)
I hold me breath when I walk by people who smoke. I also do this when I smell things I feel I shouldn’t breath fearing they might be poisonous.

Tomophobia (Fear of surgical operations.) Topophobia (Fear of certain places or situations, such as stage fright.) Toxiphobia (Fear of poison or of being accidently poisoned.) Vaccinophobia (Fear of vaccination.) Virginitiphobia (Fear of rape.) Fear of elevators,
when I was little, I was with my sister and brother in elevator at a hotel. We where leaving because there was a bad ice storm coming and we wanted to get home before the roads got too slick. My mom had told us to go wait in the lobby. On the way down the power went out. I remember it going dark then a little light came on.

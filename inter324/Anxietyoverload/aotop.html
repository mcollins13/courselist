Achluophobia (fear of darkness)
I used to not be able to go up on the second floor of my house during the night because it was dark up there. I still don’t like walking home at night.
Acrophobia (fear of heights)
one time, my sister and I took an elevator up to a restaurant at the top of a hotel. When the doors opened all I could see were windows upon windows and the city far below. I couldn’t move no matter how hard I tried. In the end my sister and I went back down in the elevator and we went somewhere else to eat.
Pseudodysphagia (Fear of choking) Anthropophobia (Fear of people or society.) Arachnephobia (Fear of spiders) Aviophobia (Fear of flying.)
I have only flown in a plane once when I was child but I was to little to remember. I haven’t been on plane since and honestly I think the only way I would get on a plane is if someone knocked me out.
Ballistophobia (Fear of missiles or bullets.) Cardiophobia (Fear of the heart.)
I’m always worried it just going to stop. My mother told that when I was kid I used to put my hand over my heart to see if was still beating.
Catagelophobia (Fear of being ridiculed.) Chemophobia (Fear of chemicals or working with chemicals.) Electrophobia (Fear of electricity.)
When I was younger I couldn’t plug or unplug cords in fear that I would get electrocuted.
Gerascophobia (Fear of growing old.) Hemophobia (Fear of blood.) Hoplophobia (Fear of firearms.) Hylephobia (fear of epilepsy.)
I don’t like flashing lights because I’m afraid of going into a seizure.
Iatrophobia (Fear of going to the doctor or of doctors.) Lockiophobia (Fear of childbirth.) Monopathophobia (Fear of definite disease.)
I struggle with this one a lot. I can’t stand to be around people who talk about getting something and what they experienced. I’m always worried I’m going to get what they got.
Nosocomephobia (Fear of hospitals.)
One time when I was in elementary school my class took a tour of a hospital. I was already nervous about hospitals but they showed x-rays of some bones and I started loosing strength in my legs. My mom was there and so I clung to her pants legs for support. She was bit upset with me because apparently I was causing her pants to slide down so she had to pull me up with her arms to keep her pants on.
Pedophobia (Fear of children.)
I feel really awkward around really young kids. I’m never sure what they’re going to do or how act around them. Also they always seem to sticking their hands in there mouths and I’m not keen on germs.
Social Phobia (Fear of being evaluated negatively in social situations.) Spacephobia (Fear of outer space.) Spermatophobia (Fear of germs.)
I was the kid that if you told me I had germs on my hands I would go wash them immediately.
Spheksophobia (Fear of wasps.) Thanatophobia (Fear of death or dying.)
This is my top fear. If you haven’t notice most of my fears are caused by things that I feel could possibly kill me.
Tomophobia (Fear of surgical operations.) Virginitiphobia (Fear of rape.) Fear of allergic reaction
I’m not allergic to anything so far but I’m worried that I will eventually be allergic to something. This makes trying new things really hard. So don’t be offended if turn down food from you.
